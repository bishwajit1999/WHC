All On Four and Pterygoid

The “all-on-four” treatment concept was developed to maximise the use of available remnant bone in atrophic jaws, allowing immediate function and avoiding regenerative procedures that increase the treatment costs and patient morbidity, as well as the complications inherent to these procedures.
